The Heritage of Sagwan Wood

For over three generations, we have been crafting premium furniture from the finest Sagwan (Teak) wood. Our legacy began in 1965 when our founder discovered the exceptional qualities of this remarkable timber.

Sagwan wood is renowned for its natural oils, exceptional durability, and beautiful grain patterns. It's naturally resistant to insects, decay, and weather, making it the perfect choice for furniture that lasts generations.
